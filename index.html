what
is called art?

Art, also called visual art, is a visual object or experience consciously created through an expression of skill or imagination.

7 types of art

Art can be categorized into seven major types: painting, sculpture, architecture, music, dance, theater, and literature. Each form expresses human creativity and emotion in unique ways. From visual to performing arts, they enrich culture, inspire imagination, and foster communication, shaping the world we live in.
